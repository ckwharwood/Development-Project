The Best Horror Movies According to Jesse

My top 3 movies of all-time.

As Above So Below

Such an original concept. Great scares. The fear follows me today.

The Exorcist

The entire franchise takes you on an unforgettable journey and offers you so much in the individual stories and the overall lore.

Tarot

This movie was fantastic but I also just could not remember the titles of any other movies I've watched recently.
